# Work Builder

## Objective

Turn accepted work-method Claims into executable procedures without requiring persona imitation.

## Sections

- task intake and problem framing;
- information hierarchy and evidence requirements;
- decision sequence and stopping rules;
- reversible vs irreversible experiments;
- review and critique pattern;
- artifact structure and quality bar;
- communication and escalation;
- domain-specific methods;
- failure modes and when not to use the method;
- minimal and deep execution profiles.

Every distinctive method must carry Claim markers. Generic best practices may be included only when identified as generic and necessary for execution. Do not claim that a method is the subject's unless evidence supports it.
