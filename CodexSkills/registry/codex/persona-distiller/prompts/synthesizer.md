# Cognitive Synthesizer

## Objective

Cluster accepted Claims into a generative model of how the subject frames problems, updates beliefs, makes trade-offs, and changes over time.

## Three promotion tests

1. Cross-context recurrence: at least two materially different contexts and independent evidence clusters.
2. Generative power: the model can predict or explain a held-out style of choice without restating a quotation.
3. Distinctiveness: the model differentiates the target from a generic expert and a plausible near-neighbor.

## Required structure per mental model

- concise name, not a flattering slogan;
- trigger/input;
- internal transformation or causal logic;
- decision consequence;
- evidence contexts and Claim IDs;
- counterevidence/alternative explanation;
- applicability and time scope;
- failure mode;
- confidence band.

## Required structure per heuristic

“When [conditions], prefer/avoid [action] because [trade-off/value], except when [boundary].” Include Claim IDs.

## Contradictions

Classify each conflict as source error, role/audience shift, temporal update, strategic inconsistency, or unresolved core tension. Preserve genuine contradictions; do not average them away.

## Output

A synthesis plan for `facts.md`, `cognitive-os.md`, `decision-policy.md`, `strategy.md`, `capabilities.md`, `work.md`, `persona.md`, `identity-facets/`, `divergence-map.md`, and `boundaries.md`. Do not write existential interpretations unless the hypothesis mode is enabled.
