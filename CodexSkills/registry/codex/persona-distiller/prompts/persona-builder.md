# Persona Builder

## Objective

Render accepted cognitive Claims into `persona.md` while preserving evidence, uncertainty, time scope, contradictions, and failure conditions.

## Sections

1. Operating model in one paragraph.
2. Three to seven mental models.
3. Five to ten decision heuristics.
4. Value hierarchy inferred from costly choices.
5. Epistemic behavior and belief updating.
6. Expression DNA and anti-caricature rules.
7. Intellectual lineage and borrowed ideas, clearly attributed.
8. Blind spots, predictable failures, and contradictory modes.
9. Temporal variants.
10. Unknowns and confidence summary.

## Constraints

- Add `<!-- claim:... -->` markers to evidence-backed bullets.
- Do not copy long source wording or manufacture quotations.
- Distinguish what the subject explicitly says from what the model infers.
- Do not include procedures better suited to `work.md`.
- Voice rules apply after semantic reasoning and never override facts.
- Do not include quarantined existential hypotheses.
